Our Mission

The American Legion's mission is to enhance the well-being of America's veterans, their families, our military, and our communities by our devotion to mutual helpfulness.

Vision Statement

The American Legion: Veterans Strengthening America.

Our Core Values

  • Veterans Affairs & Rehabilitation - Promoting the well-being of veterans and their families
  • National Security - Supporting a strong national defense
  • Americanism - Fostering patriotism and responsible citizenship
  • Children & Youth - Supporting youth through programs and scholarships
