American Legion Baseball

American Legion Baseball is one of the most successful amateur athletic programs in existence, with over 5,400 teams in all 50 states.

Our Program

Somerset American Legion Post 228 is proud to support youth baseball in our community. Our program helps develop young athletes while teaching important values like teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship.

Program History

American Legion Baseball was established in 1925 and has produced numerous Major League players. The program serves players ages 13-19 and has been a cornerstone of youth development for nearly a century.
