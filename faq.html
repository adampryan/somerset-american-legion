Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the American Legion?

Membership is open to all U.S. veterans who served at least one day of active duty during eligible war eras and were honorably discharged. Visit our eligibility page for full details.

How much are membership dues?

Contact our post at 508-679-2380 for current dues information.

Can I rent the hall for a private event?

Yes! Our hall is available for weddings, parties, and other events. See our hall rental page for details.

When are meetings held?

General meetings are typically held the first Tuesday of each month. Contact us for the current schedule.

How can I support the post without being a veteran?

Non-veterans can join the Legion Auxiliary or Sons of the American Legion. You can also donate or volunteer.

Have more questions? Contact us - we're happy to help!
