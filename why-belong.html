Why You Belong

As a veteran, you've already proven your commitment to service. The American Legion offers you a way to continue that service while connecting with others who share your experiences.

Continue Your Service

Your military service may have ended, but your desire to serve hasn't. The Legion provides countless opportunities to give back to your community and fellow veterans.

Make Your Voice Heard

With nearly 2 million members, the American Legion is a powerful advocate for veterans on Capitol Hill. Your membership adds strength to that voice.

Find Your Community

There's something special about being around people who understand what you've been through. The Legion provides that sense of belonging and camaraderie.

Help Fellow Veterans

Many veterans struggle with the transition to civilian life. Through the Legion, you can help guide others through challenges you may have faced yourself.
