Sons of the American Legion

The Sons of the American Legion (SAL) is a program for male descendants of American Legion members and veterans.

Who Can Join

  • Sons and grandsons of Legion members
  • Sons and grandsons of deceased veterans who served during eligible war eras
  • Male descendants adopted by Legion members or eligible veterans

Our Mission

SAL members support the Legion's programs and continue the tradition of service to veterans, military, and community. Members participate in community service, fundraising, and patriotic activities.
