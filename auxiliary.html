American Legion Auxiliary

The American Legion Auxiliary is the world's largest women's patriotic service organization.

Who Can Join

  • Wives, mothers, daughters, sisters, and granddaughters of Legion members
  • Female veterans eligible for Legion membership
  • Female descendants of veterans who served during eligible war eras

What We Do

Auxiliary members volunteer millions of hours annually in service to veterans, military, and their communities. Activities include:

  • Supporting hospitalized veterans
  • Scholarships and education programs
  • Community service projects
  • Girls State citizenship program
