About Somerset American Legion Post 228

American Legion Post No. 228 Somerset, MA
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

The American Legion is a nonpartisan, not-for-profit organization with great political influence perpetuated by its grass-roots involvement in the legislation process from local districts to Capitol Hill.

The American Legion was chartered and incorporated by Congress in 1919 as a patriotic veterans organization devoted to mutual helpfulness. Legionnaires' sense of obligation to community, state, and nation drives an honest advocacy for veterans in Washington.

The Legion stands behind the issues most important to the nation's veteran's community, backed by resolutions passed by volunteer leadership.
