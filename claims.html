VA Claims Assistance

The American Legion provides free assistance with VA claims and benefits to all veterans, regardless of Legion membership.

How We Help

  • Filing disability compensation claims
  • Appeals of denied claims
  • Pension applications
  • Healthcare enrollment
  • Education benefits
  • Home loan certificates

Our Service Officers

American Legion Service Officers are trained and accredited to help veterans navigate the VA system. This service is completely free.

Get Help

Contact our post to connect with a service officer, or visit the American Legion Service Officer locator.
